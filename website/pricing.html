Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really no catch?

No catch. It's AGPL-3.0 licensed. Read every line, fork it, run it on a potato. Just keep your changes open too — that's the deal.

What about the Pro plan?

There is no Pro plan. There's just the app. You download it and it works. Revolutionary business model.

Do you offer volume discounts?

We're pleased to offer 100% off for any number of seats. This applies retroactively and in perpetuity.

Can I talk to sales?

No. There is no sales team. If you need to talk to someone, open a GitHub issue. We might respond.
